Those two projects were a final part in our Video production class. They were filmed with Nikon D3500. The process of working on those videos took several steps. First one was Pre-production It usually begins once the script is finished and involves the director, cinematographer, producers, first assistant director, production managers, production coordinators, and location scouts. The actual Production process took 2-3 days and was very enjoable. Especially while filming the food and then eating it!

Pre-production steps

  1. Lock the shooting script
  2. Finalize the budget
  3. Form a new company (not always applicable)
  4. Hire key department heads
  5. Break down the script
  6. Storyboard and shot list the scenes
